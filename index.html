About me

I am a Senior Research Scientist at Google Cloud AI Research based in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Previously, I did my DPhil (the Oxford way of saying PhD) in the Machine Learning Research Group, Department of Engineering Science, University of Oxford.

💡 Preprint: Reasoning-SQL
New paper Apr 2025
We applied DeepSeek R1-style GRPO post-training to obtain Reasoning-SQL 14B, achieving SoTA results on par or better than much larger models on the BIRD benchmark. Check the preprint here.
💡 Preprint: Automating multi-agent designs
New paper Feb 2025
Our new work on automating prompt and topology design in multi-agent LLM systems is now available as an arXiv preprint!
📄 ICLR acceptance: Self-improving long-context reasoners
New acceptance Feb 2025
Our work, From Few to Many: Self-Improving Many-Shot Reasoners Through Iterative Optimization and Generation, was accepted at ICLR 2025.
📄 3 papers accepted at NeurIPS 2024 + 1 paper accepted at EMNLP 2024!
New acceptance Sep 2024
  • A comprehensive study of instructions and demonstrations in automatic prompt design, which also powers the recently launched Vertex AI Prompt Optimizer [Google Cloud Blog] [Google for Developers Blog] [Vertex AI Documentation];
  • Unstructured Query Engine (UQE), an LLM-powered database engine on unstructured data;
  • GraphComBO [code], a novel Bayesian optimization framework operating over node subsets in graphs.
  • ZePO [code] which aligns LLM-as-a-judge with human preferences with intelligent prompting was accepted at EMNLP 2024 as a main paper.
